About

Pneumonia is an inflammation of the air sacs in the lungs (alveoli) and the surrounding tissue. It often leads to a sudden high fever, feeling unwell, a cough, and shortness of breath.

Types of Pneumonia

Pneumonia can be caused by a variety of different pathogens, including bacteria, viruses, and fungi.

Diagnosis of Pneumonia

Diagnosing pneumonia involves a physical examination, listening for crackling sounds in the lungs, and tests such as chest X-rays.
